The story behind "Storm-Riders"

"Storm Riders" began 10 years ago when five friends got togather and wanted to do what they loved most, riding bikes and helping others find out the beauty of bike riding. It all started with a small studio for fixing bikes and slowly but surly it got to be what it is today. 10 years later it became one of three major bike studios in Israel.
